Blue Orchard Insights – FAQs

Answers to common questions about how Blue Orchard Insights works.

What is Blue Orchard Insights?
Blue Orchard Insights is an app that helps users reflect on conversations by generating transcripts, summaries, and suggested follow-up questions from audio.
How does the app work?
Users upload or record audio. The app processes that audio to generate transcripts and reflective insights designed to support personal understanding and growth.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your content is handled according to our privacy policy. We do not sell your data. You can review full details here: Privacy Policy.
Does the app provide therapy or professional advice?
No. Blue Orchard Insights is a reflection tool only. It does not provide therapy, diagnosis, legal advice, or medical advice.
Who owns the content I upload?
You retain ownership of your content. You are responsible for ensuring you have the right to upload and use any recordings or materials.
Can I delete my data?
Yes. You can delete your sessions or request account deletion by contacting us at info@BlueOrchardInnovations.com.
Do I need consent to record conversations?
Yes. You are responsible for complying with all applicable laws and obtaining any required consent before recording or uploading audio.
Will the app always be accurate?
No. Transcripts and AI-generated insights may not be perfectly accurate and should not be relied upon as factual or professional conclusions.
How can I contact support?
You can contact us at: info@BlueOrchardInnovations.com.
