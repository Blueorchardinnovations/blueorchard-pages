Overview

Blue Orchard TrackFit AI solves a common creator problem: music needs to be shorter, but it should not sound chopped, abrupt, or unfinished.

The app is designed to preserve the musical identity of a track by protecting intros, endings, transitions, and flow while shortening the middle or rebuilding the structure.

Best for:
podcast intros, social clips, transition music, reflection beds, outros, and short-form video audio.
Main goal:
shorten audio without destroying the feel of the original track.
Core workflow:
upload audio, choose duration, preview, then share or download.

Quick Shorten

Quick Shorten is the fast trimming workflow. It removes material from the middle while preserving the intro and outro so the track still feels complete.

Key settings

  • Target Length: Type a duration or choose preset chips such as Radio Edit, Social Clip, or Podcast Bed.
  • Crossfade Slider: Adjusts transition smoothness from 0.5 to 5 seconds.
  • Natural Mode: Uses smoother fade curves for less mechanical cuts.
  • Smart Cuts: Attempts to snap cuts to beat boundaries so the edit sounds musical.
  • Preserve Intro & Outro: Protects the opening and ending identity of the track.
  • Advanced Sliders: Fine tune how much intro and outro to keep.
Example: A podcaster can turn a two-minute music track into a 12-second intro, a 20-second reflection bed, or a 6-second transition without manually editing the waveform.

Smart Remix

Smart Remix is the intelligent reconstruction mode. It analyzes musical structure such as intro, verse, chorus, bridge, and outro, then rebuilds the track around actual song sections instead of making a simple middle cut.

Trailer editors:
Create short versions that preserve rise, climax, and release.
Ministry media:
Create countdown or transition music that still feels natural.
Podcast creators:
Build music beds that evolve instead of looping awkwardly.

Presets Screen

Presets give users one-tap setting templates. Tap a preset and the app loads the recommended trimming settings on the Main Screen.

Free presets

  • Radio Edit: 60% of original length with a 2.5 second crossfade.
  • Social Clip: 60 seconds with a 2.0 second crossfade.
  • Podcast Bed: 90 seconds with a 3.0 second crossfade.

Creator presets

  • Intro Music: 12 seconds.
  • Transition Bed: 6 seconds.
  • Reflection Bed: 20 seconds.
  • Outro Music: 10 seconds.

Podcast Builder — Creator Only

Podcast Builder allows users to upload background music and an optional voice recording, then export a polished stereo MP3 segment ready for a podcast editor.

Useful for

  • Podcast intros and outros.
  • Segment transitions.
  • Spoken reflections and devotionals.
  • Motivational audio clips.
  • Branded short-form audio content.
