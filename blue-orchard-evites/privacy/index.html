Privacy Policy for TrackFit AI

Effective Date: May 17, 2026

TrackFit AI is owned and operated by Blue Orchard Innovations, Inc. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the TrackFit AI mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: such as your email address or login identifier if account access is required.
  • Uploaded Audio Files: audio files you choose to upload for processing inside the app.
  • Usage Information: information about app interactions, feature usage, export activity, subscription status, and performance.
  • Purchase Information: subscription status and transaction-related data processed through Apple App Store subscriptions and RevenueCat.
  • Device and Diagnostic Information: crash logs, device type, app version, operating system version, and performance data.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide audio processing and creator workflow features.
  • Allow users to upload, process, preview, and export audio.
  • Manage subscription access and premium features.
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Detect technical issues, bugs, and failed processing events.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Protect the app from abuse, excessive usage, or unauthorized access.

3. Uploaded Audio Content

TrackFit AI does not provide copyrighted music or audio content. Users upload their own audio files and are responsible for ensuring they have the rights or permission to use any uploaded content.

Uploaded files may be processed temporarily to generate previews, exports, waveform data, timing information, or other app functionality. We do not claim ownership of user-uploaded audio.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

TrackFit AI uses Apple App Store subscriptions for paid access. Subscription purchases, renewals, cancellations, and refunds are handled by Apple.

We use RevenueCat to help validate subscription status and manage access to premium features. RevenueCat may process transaction identifiers, subscription status, product identifiers, and related purchase data.

5. Third-Party Services

TrackFit AI may use third-party services for functions such as:

  • Subscription validation and entitlement management.
  • Authentication.
  • Analytics and app performance monitoring.
  • Crash reporting and diagnostics.
  • Cloud processing or storage, where applicable.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, maintain subscription access, support users, comply with legal obligations, prevent abuse, and improve functionality.

Uploaded audio files may be deleted after processing or after a limited retention period unless saved as part of a user-accessible project history feature.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help operate the app, process subscriptions, authenticate users, monitor performance, or provide support.

We may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users, Blue Orchard Innovations, Inc., or others.

8. Children’s Privacy

TrackFit AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
